How to Enter

  1. 1Download the appropriate colouring sheet for your child’s age category.
  2. 2Complete the colouring sheet, or submit original artwork for Ages 12–17.
  3. 3Complete and sign the Parent/Guardian Consent Form.
  4. 4Email your completed artwork and signed consent form to socialcommittee@oeservices.ca.
Entries must be received by July 29, 2026.

Contest Rules

  • Contest is open to children of employees currently at OE Utility Services.
  • One entry per child.
  • Ages 1–11 must use the company-provided colouring sheet.
  • Ages 12–17 may submit either the company-provided colouring sheet or original artwork based on the contest theme.
  • A completed Parent/Guardian Consent Form must accompany every entry.
  • Entries must be received by July 29, 2026.
  • Judges’ decisions are final.
  • Permission is required to display artwork.

Artwork Gallery

All finished artwork will be displayed in the gallery for employee viewing and voting.

Entries appear after the July 29 submission deadline — each shown with the artist’s first name and age only.
